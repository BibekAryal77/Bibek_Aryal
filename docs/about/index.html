About Me

I am a mechanical engineering Ph.D. student at the University of Missouri–Columbia. My research focuses on two-phase flow, flow boiling, CFD simulation, and thermal management. I am interested in combining experiments, numerical simulation, and machine learning to improve heat transfer prediction and design.

Research Interests

  • Two-phase flow visualization
  • Flow boiling in microchannels and minichannels
  • CFD simulation using ANSYS Fluent, COMSOL, and OpenFOAM
  • Curved channel and U-bend two-phase flow
  • Heat transfer enhancement
  • Machine learning for heat transfer coefficient prediction
  • Physics-informed neural networks
  • Electronics cooling and thermal management
